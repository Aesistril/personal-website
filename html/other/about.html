About Me

Hi there! I'm a classic stereotypical coding teenager from Turkey! I love retro systems, low level software, linux and unix-like systems and electronics. And I think making lists are more effective for describing something so lets make one:

  • Favourite Linux Distros: Ubuntu for desktops Debian for servers and Bedrock Linux for fantasy
  • Favourite Editor: VSCode but I don't want Microsoft spyware so I use VSCodium (Great IDE! Thank you so much Microsoft! I hate you)
  • Favourite Language: Python
  • I Hate:
    • Javascript
    • Chromium
    • Soydevs
    • Electron
    • I only like community based social media platforms like reddit and I hate person-based social media (Like F*cebook and Instagram)
    • Mac, Windows, Ios and all the other oversimplified OSes
    • Microsoft, Apple and probably a 95% of big companies
  • Favourite Games: Portal 2, Minecraft, Forza Horizon, Valorant, Teardown, Kerbal Space Program, Superliminal, Mirrors Edge, Stardew Valley, Satisfactory
  • I Liked It But Not My Favourite: Astroneer, Cyberpunk 2077, Crysis, Superhot
  • Things You Can Play While Waiting: Geometry Dash, osu!
  • Crap (Don't @ me!): FarCry 5
